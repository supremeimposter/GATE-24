# Sequential Circuits

- In sequential circuits, state = output = stored data.


> [!convention] 
> $Q^+ = Q_n = Q_{t + 1}$


- In sequential circuits, synchronous means **in sync with clock** and asynchronous means **not in sync with clock**.
