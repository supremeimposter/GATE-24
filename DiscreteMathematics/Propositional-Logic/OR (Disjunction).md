- Inclusive OR means you can either do this or that and **ALSO** both.
- It is also a binary connective.

| p   | q   | p or q |
| --- | --- | ------ |
| F   | F   | F      |
| F   | T   | T      |
| T   | F   | T      |
| T   | T   | T       |
