# Semi-Group


## Power of an element in a Semi-Group
- For a semi-group $(S, \star)$,  $\forall a \in S$
	- $a^n = a^{n - 1} \star a, \; \text{ for } n \ge 1$
- No negative power and 0th power since there are no inverse or identity elements in a semi-group.
